What to do in Amelia Island?

Amelia Island is home to Fernandina Beach, a colorful downtown district with boutique shops, ice cream parlors, and restaurants. Amelia is the annual host of the Amelia Island Concours d'Elegance, the Amelia Island Jazz Festival, Amelia Island Film Festival, the Southern Lardo Festival, the Isle of Eight Flags Shrimp Festival, and the Amelia Island Chamber Music Festival. For the outdoor lovers, Amelia offers kayaking and canoeing adventure, sunset boat cruises, fishing charters and tours, nature trails to explore, and abundant natural wildlife to see. We highly recommend Amelia Island Kayak Excursions for guided kayak tours around Amelia Island. And for more romantic or special outdoor occasions, Élevé Events is a new luxury picnic and micro event company serving Amelia Island and Nassau County with unique and stylish events and picnics.
